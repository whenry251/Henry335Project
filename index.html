ABOUT ME

NWMSU senior studying IDM: New Media. I should probably be a stakeholder in Taco Bell for all the spicy potato soft tacos I have consumed.

If you didn't notice the big text earlier, I'm Whitney Henry, a hopeful social media strategist with a love for connecting with the customer and finding out what drives the most engagement. I dabble in design and other things that I list in my skills, but my real passion lies in managing accounts and working at the ground level to make sure the connection between consumer and social media personality is real, in depth, and above all, not over-saturated with gimmicks.

I've got real-world experience managing channels for two different small town businesses in Southwest Iowa, and I inadvertently provide content consultation for many more. They ask me one question about my major and it sends me into an endless ramble on user experience, engagement, and the best techniques to optimize your page. Whoops?
